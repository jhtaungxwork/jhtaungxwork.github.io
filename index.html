About Us

Bleu Psychiatry implements a holistic, individual centered approach to treating children, teens and adults by providing the highest level of care in evidence-based treatment. We provide a comprehensive evaluation and evaluate for different mental health conditions while addressing the individual's physical well-being and environmental factors.

We work to inform and empower the individual and for children and teens we work towards optimizing the family unit. Our team values working and collaborating with providers from numerous fields.

Navigating mental health services can be overwhelming, our team can assist in connecting individuals and families to treatment.

Services

Diagnostic Evaluations

Our comprehensive diagnostic evaluations are designed to uncover the root causes of mental health challenges. By using evidence-based assessments, we help identify specific conditions or concerns, guiding a personalized plan for your mental well-being. Whether you're experiencing anxiety, depression, or other mental health issues, our evaluations provide clarity and the foundation for effective treatment.

Medication Management

Managing mental health conditions often requires the right balance of medication. Our medication management service ensures you receive the correct prescriptions, dosages, and adjustments over time. We work closely with you to monitor your progress, minimize side effects, and optimize your treatment for the best possible outcomes.

Lifestyle Recommendations

Your mental health is deeply connected to your lifestyle. We offer customized lifestyle recommendations to improve your overall well-being. From nutrition and exercise to sleep and stress management, our holistic approach helps you make meaningful changes that positively impact your mental health and enhance your quality of life.

Individual Psychotherapy

Our licensed providers offer individualized psychotherapy in a safe, supportive space to help you navigate life's challenges. Using evidence-based techniques, we empower you to build resilience, develop healthier coping strategies, and create lasting positive change.

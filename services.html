Services

We provide both in-person or tele-health comprehensive psychiatric assessments of children, adolescents, and adults. When indicated we may recommend medication and other resources including therapy.

Fees

We are out-of-network providers, which means we do not accept insurance. However, we can provide a superbill upon request which you may submit to your insurance for reimbursement. If you are interested in using your insurance, we recommend you contact your insurance company to inquire about your out-of-network benefits.

ABOUT US

LUME Psychiatry is a private practice in Los Angeles. Our team focuses on providing a holistic approach to treating children, adolescents, and adults.
